Anti-Pump & Dump

What follows most presale is a pump in price and massive dumping from whales and bots. Hypersonic token is loaded with features that strictly manages the behaviour and experience of investors.

The token has dynamic sell fees ranging from 70% to 0 fees thereby making it cost 70% fee to sell at the first day of getting the tokens. Check here for more info.

Anti-Whale

In regular tokens, an early bird can own up to 20% of the total supply or even more, in most cases, when he dumps those tokens, the project would be massively affected.

Hypersonic token is embedded with anti-whale investor and transaction functions. This helps to minimise price impact on sell transactions and control the FUD.

A waller cannot buy or hold more than 5000 Hypersonic tokens. A transaction (sell / transfer) cannot exceed 1000 tokens at a time.
